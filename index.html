Who We Are

Jubo Bangla Samiti is a youth-led organisation founded with the dream of creating a stronger, cleaner and more prosperous West Bengal.


Our movement seeks to inspire individuals to actively participate in social welfare, environmental awareness, education and community development. Through unity, integrity and collective action, we aspire to contribute towards building a peaceful and progressive Bengal that future generations can be proud of.


Though our journey has only just begun, our commitment towards serving society and encouraging positive change remains unwavering. We believe that even small steps taken with sincerity can pave the way towards a brighter tomorrow.

Our vision extends beyond the present. We aspire to encourage social awareness, strengthen environmental protection, empower the youth and contribute towards building stronger communities that uphold the values of dignity, responsibility and service.

Founder

Adhiraj Dev Varman Ghosh

Founder, Jubo Bangla Samiti | Youth Leader & Social Activist


Adhiraj Dev Varman Ghosh is the Founder of Jubo Bangla Samiti and a dedicated youth leader committed to community development and public service. Through grassroots initiatives and active community participation, he seeks to inspire positive change and encourage responsible citizenship among the youth.


Born on 8 October 2008, he completed his education in the Arts stream from Vivekananda English Academy. He represents a new generation of young leaders driven by service, integrity and a vision for a better society.


His mission is to build stronger, cleaner and more responsive communities through responsible leadership, social awareness and collective action. Guided by the values of unity, honesty and public welfare, he aims to inspire young people to actively participate in nation-building and contribute towards the progress and prosperity of West Bengal.
